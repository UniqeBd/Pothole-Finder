About Pothhole Finder

Pothhole Finder was created to make it easier for citizens to report road hazards and potholes directly to local authorities. Our goal is to improve road safety and infrastructure maintenance by ensuring every road issue gets noticed and fixed quickly.

Our Mission

To make reporting road hazards and potholes simple and transparent for safer roads.

Our Vision

To build safer roads and better infrastructure through active citizen participation in road maintenance reporting.
